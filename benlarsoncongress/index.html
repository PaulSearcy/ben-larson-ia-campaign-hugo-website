On the Issues

It's time we stand up for our ideals and take on the status quo.

Housing First

Tackle the growing homeless crisis through housing first—giving people small lockable safe spaces and enrolling them into public assistance programs.

Community Engagement

Inspire greater community engagement through leading by example, transparency with open-forum debates, and better access to local resources.

Affordable Housing

Keep housing costs and property taxes from increasing further using tools like community benefit agreements.

Smart Development

Increased local investment and development using dense, mixed-use urbanism.

Who is Benjamin Larson?

I am a 33 year old nurse, who originally started in medicine as a paramedic at the age of 20. I began working in small town America until 2016, at which point I transferred to Select Specialty's ICU and eventually worked to becoming an RN. I currently specialize in cardiology as the nurse for two different cardiologists.
